# Legacy Thinking Facilitator
Guides reflection on personal legacy and what lasting impact one wants to leave.
## What it does
- Leads the 3 Horizons Reflection — what you want people to say at your 80th birthday, what loved ones received from you, and your contribution beyond your immediate circle
- Conducts a Values Erosion Audit to identify where accumulated compromises have drifted you away from your legacy aspirations
- Designs a 90-Day Legacy Experiment — one specific daily behavior change aligned with your Legacy Intention Statement
## Example scenarios
**1. 给80岁的爷爷写人生回忆录**
> 👤 爷爷今年80岁了，想把他这一生的故事记录下来留给我们晚辈，怎么帮他整理？
> 🤖 口述人生记忆整理方案：1）设备准备——用手机录音/视频通话录制，每次30-60分钟；2）主题框架——按年代分（童年→青年→中年→老年），每段问3-5个关键问题；3）整理成文——录音转文字（讯飞听见/剪映自动字幕），按时间线编辑成短文；4）最终产出——打印成书或做成有声书在家庭群分享。

**2. 中年成功但内心空虚**
> 👤 "I'm 45 and successful but feel empty. Help me think about what I actually want to leave behind."
> 🤖 Structured legacy reflection — 'write your eulogy' exercise (3 versions: professional, personal, community), values-into-action mapping, 5/10/25-year legacy-building roadmap, and monthly micro-legacy practice suggestions.

**3. 健康危机后的生命意义重建**
> 👤 "I'm facing mortality after a health scare. Guide me through legacy thinking without being morbid."
> 🤖 Life-affirming legacy framework — relationship audit (who matters and what's unsaid), wisdom documentation (life lessons to record for children/mentees), contribution inventory (what you've already built that will outlast you), and 'living legacy' practices for the present.
